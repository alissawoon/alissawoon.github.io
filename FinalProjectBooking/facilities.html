A realm of recreation and relaxation.
Refreshing Dip.

Whether on business or leisure, you are welcome to enjoy a refreshing dip in the infinity swimming pool or simply lounge by the pool with a refreshing beverage and appetizing snacks while you admire the beauty of the city.

Fitness come first.
Be your own role model.

When it comes to maintaining a fit and healthy physique, consistency is key. Get into your workout attire and head over to the O2 Gym that is equipped with cardiovascular and stamina building exercise machines to help you maintain your daily fitness regime. The O2 Gym is strategically located beside the main adult swimming pool in case you want to combine your workout with a good swim. You may also choose to adjourn to the sauna located nearby as it is known to aid muscle recovery.

Teamwork Games.
Fast Moving.

Basketball is one of the most popular sports in the world and one of the most widely viewed. It is a team sport that involves two teams of five active players each trying to score points against one another by throwing a ball through a 300 cm (10 feet) high hoop (the ‘basket’) under organised rules. It is played on both indoor and outdoor courts.
